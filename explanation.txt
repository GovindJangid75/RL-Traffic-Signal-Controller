# Project Explanation: Traffic RL v2 (Hinglish)

## 🚦 Problem Statement (Hums kya solve kr re hain?)
Traffic signals pe fixed timers (jaise 30 seconds wait) hamre cities me congestion create krte hain. Kabhi ek side bilkul khali hoti hai fir bhi signal red rhta hai. 
Is project ka goal hai ek **AI Agent** bnana jo traffic ko dekh kr "real-time" me decide kre ki konsi lane ki light green honi chahiye, taaki:
1. Gaadiyan jaldi clear hon (Waiting time kam ho).
2. Emergency vehicles (Ambulance etc.) ko priority mile.
3. Fuel waste aur emissions kam hon.

---

## 🏗️ Architecture (Kon kya kr rha hai?)

### 1. `env/traffic_env.py` (The Environment)
Ye hamara "Simulated World" hai. 
- Isme 4 lanes hain (North, South, East, West).
- Ye handle krta hai: 
    - **Weather**: Barish ya tufan me gaadiyan dhire chalti hain.
    - **Time of Day**: Subah aur sham ko "Rush Hour" hota hai.
    - **Vehicle Types**: Bus aur truck slow hote hain, bike fast.
- **State**: Agent ko batata hai ki har lane me kitni gaadiyan hain.
- **Reward**: Agar AI gaadi clear krta hai to use Points milte hain (+ve reward). Agar traffic jam hota hai ya pollution badhta hai to points kat'te hain (-ve reward).

### 2. `agent/q_learning.py` (Tabular AI)
Ye ek "Basic Brain" hai jo ek **Q-Table** use krta hai. 
- Ye table me likhta rhta hai ki "State A" me "Action B" lene se kitna fayda hua. 
- Small scenarios ke liye ye bahut fast aur effective hai.

### 3. `agent/dqn_agent.py` (Deep AI - DQN)
Jab traffic bahut complex ho jata hai (continuous values), tab Q-Table chota pad jata hai. 
- Isme ek **Neural Network** (Brain) hota hai.
- Ye **PyTorch** use krta hai pattern seekhne ke liye.
- Ye bade level ke traffic management ke liye perfect hai.

### 4. `train.py` (The Coaching)
Ye script agent ko environment me "hazaron bar" (episodes) practice krwati hai. Successive practice se AI smarter banta jata hai.

### 5. `app.py` (Visual Dashboard)
Ye user interface hai jo **Streamlit** pe bana hai. 
- Isme aap live dekh skte ho ki AI kaise decision le rha hai.
- Ek interactive **JS Canvas** hai jisme gaadiyan chalti hui dikhti hain.
- Graphs dikhate hain ki AI vs Manual Timer me kitna difference hai.

---

## 🛠️ Kaise kaam kr rha hai? (Workflow)
1. **Reset**: Environment start hota hai, kuch random gaadiyan lanes me aati hain.
2. **Observe**: AI dekhta hai ki North/South me kitni gaadiyan hain.
3. **Action**: AI decide krta hai "0" (NS Green) ya "1" (EW Green).
4. **Reward**: Environment batata hai ki kitni gaadiyan clear hui aur AI ko reward data hai.
5. **Learn**: AI apne brain (Q-Table ya NN) ko update krta hai taaki agli baar aur behtar decision le sake.
6. **Repeat**: Ye loop 100-200 baar (steps) chalta hai har episode me.

Summary: Hum Reinforcement Learning se traffic signals ko "Smart" bana rahe hain jo khud environment ke hisab se adapt ho sakein!
